Features

Built for how you watch.

Overlay or docked, full third-party emotes, chat delay, and live highlights — all on the player.

Twitch chat on YouTube

Live Twitch chat with badges, colors and case-correct names — rendered with native Twitch emotes plus full 7TV, BetterTTV and FrankerFaceZ sets, right over the YouTube or Kick player.

7TV BetterTTV FrankerFaceZ

Configurable chat delay

Sync the conversation with a delayed stream. A client-side queue from real-time up to ten minutes, adjustable from the overlay header.

12seconds
0 = real-time600s

Overlay & docked modes

Float a transparent panel over the video, or dock it inside the site's native chat frame with a tab switcher. Dial in background opacity, blur and font size until it sits perfectly either way.

Emote highlights

When enough unique viewers spam one emote in a short window, Meridian pins a marker on the YouTube seekbar at that exact moment. Click it to jump straight to the surge — or replay the highlights on the VOD afterwards.
