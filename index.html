Twitch Chat, Anywhere You Watch

Overlay live Twitch chat on YouTube and Kick — draggable, resizable, and entirely in your browser. No backend, no tracking, no account required to read.

Features

Overlay & Docked Modes

Float a transparent panel over the video, or dock it inside the site's native chat frame with a [Site | Twitch] tab switcher. Survives theater and fullscreen.

Rich Emote Support

Native Twitch emotes plus 7TV, BetterTTV, and FrankerFaceZ — global and per-channel sets, each provider individually toggleable.

Timeline Highlights

A chat-activity wave drawn over the YouTube seekbar. Emote surges are pinned as clickable markers — click to seek straight to the hype moment.

Chat Delay Sync

Dial in 0–600 s of chat delay to sync messages with a delayed stream or re-broadcast.

Auto Channel Mapping

Automatically maps YouTube channel handles and Kick slugs to the right Twitch channel. Override per-page anytime.

Private by Design

No servers, no analytics, no data leaves your browser. Read chat anonymously without connecting an account. Full privacy policy →
